3 CONSEQUENCES of SPACE TRAVEL

Can we SURVIVE in SPACE

Space travel is not without risk. Here is a list of the 3 worst physical consequences of long-duration space travel.

The WORST EFFECTS in SPACE

3. Loss of muscle mass
Dans l'espace, nos muscles ne sont pas aussi sollicités par l'attraction terrestre. Le manque de résistance peut faire diminuer notre masse musculaire jusqu'à -30%. Plus le voyage sera long, plus grave seront les effets. Au bout de 6 mois, le phénomène peut atteindre la totalité de notre corps jusqu'a atteindre nos organes. Ainsi, la pratique d'un entraînement intensif quotidien permet de limiter les risques en apesanteur.
2. Loss of sight
This phenomenon is due to the accumulation of cerebrospinal fluid in our skull. In weightlessness, the liquid collects on the orbits and deforms them progressively. A one-week trip would have no impact on our sight, but beyond 6 months the effects become irreversible.
1. Cancer risks
In space, astronauts are subject to the effects of cosmic rays. By releasing their energy, these particles modify our DNA molecules, and can cause many cancers. Their effects are comparable to radioactivity. On earth, the atmosphere protects us from these rays, but this is not the case on the moon or on mars.
