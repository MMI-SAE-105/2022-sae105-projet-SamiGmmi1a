wall-e

A film who've predict our future

What if our world ended like the one presented in the movie Wall-e. A dry and arid land, without vegetation, filled with waste. Could this future be possible? Did Wall-E predict our future? X'plore invites you to rediscover this Pixar Studios classic.

SYNOPSIS

Wall-E is a Pixar animated film released in 2008.
Alone on earth, Wall-E is one of many robots tasked with cleaning up the earth. He discovers the last trace of vegetation. It is then the meet of Eve takes place. A droid tasking to retrieve the plant discovered by Wall-E. Then follows a journey into the depths of the universe where Wall-E will find the human species that had left the earth...
