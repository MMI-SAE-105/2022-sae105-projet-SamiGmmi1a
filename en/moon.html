How will be the LIFE on the MOON?

How lunar living conditions look like?

Time zones are different than earth. A moon day represents 14 earth days, same for the nights. But this is what worries us the most. The temperature could down till -180°. With nights of 336 hours, it's impossible to create enough energy with just solar panel. But there is a solution: place the base on the south of the moon, the most exposed part to the sun.

In wich house to live?

Space companies are already thinking about the theme of the architecture of space bases. The best idea will be to build bases underground or in a crater, sheltered from cosmic rays and meteorites.
Scientists are also thinking about how these bases will be built. The 1st option could be 3D printing, replacing the plastic with cement.
The 2nd option, and the most probable, could be to inflate the houses directly on the moon, and cover the structure with lunar rock.
As to produce electricity, it will be done mainly by solar panel. But a very ambitious project is committed to building a nuclear power plant on the moon
