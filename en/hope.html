LIFE on the MOON

What if we CAN LEAVE on the MOON?

Living on the Moon is the first step in the conquest of space. Thanks to its low gravity, and the close distance to Mars, it is the ideal place for the takeoff of rockets for a possible conquest of planets.

3 important reason to return on the moon.

3. mineral resources: resources like rare stones are also present on the moon. Nothing to do with jewellery. These are metals widely used on earth, and present in large quantities on the moon. However, this mineral is an essential component in the manufacture of batteries and electric chargers.
2. Lunar water: there is also water in the form of ice. Appeared when the collision between the moon and the earth 4.5 trillion years ago. This discovery is the most important of all. Its proves that life is quite possible on the moon. Also, able to hydrate and breathe, it could be used as fuel for the take-off of the Rockets, which today operate by combustion of hydrogen and oxygen
1. Helium 3: its origin came from the ray of the sun propelled by the solar wind. Same as terrestrial helium, except it has one neutron less. Possessing a high level of energy, its produce enough to power a nuclear power plant. Only, the energy necessary to extract it and bring it to earth is 3 times greater than what it could bring. The ideal is therefore to exploit it directly a moon directly.
