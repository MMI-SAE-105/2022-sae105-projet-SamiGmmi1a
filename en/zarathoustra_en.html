ALSO SPRACHT ZARATUSTRA

The SUNRISE

Also, Sprach Zarathustra" a name that may seem unknown but whose main theme is recognizable among all the great film scores. Similar to the opening of the son of "2001, Space Odyssey" or one of the openings of "The Simpson's", three words can describe this essential music: "evolution, grandeur, universe".
Today, X'plore propose to you to learn more about a masterpiece who will stay severe in our memories as our evolution anthem.

Where this song come from?

"Also Spratch Zarathoustra" in English "Thus Spoke Zarathoustra" was composed in 1896 by Richard Strauss, in homage to the German philosopher Friedrich Nietzsche, for his book entitled similar, on the exploits of the evolution of Man.
Significance of the beginning of the human evolutionary journey from primitive status to the biped that we are today.
Strauss wanted to transcribe the idea of a progressive development of man step by step.
This oeuvre is so important of the fact that it represents all the efforts that the man put for trying of always to push the limits of his species. Today, our greatest ambition is to travel and discover the universe beyond of our sun system.
