The TRAVEL to another PLANET

Part 1
Eden
The ship moved through space in dead silence. He spun; quietly, across skies that no human had explored before. Its hull, a metallic brown, was in perfect condition. On each side was painted in large white letters the name of the ship: L'Arche...
Inside, no activity, no lights, no engine. The corridors, a series of long corridors that looked alike without ever really being identical, were plunged in dense darkness. Sometimes leading to a navigation station, a laboratory, bedrooms, a cafeteria, an engine room. No sound, except the dull and constant noise of a ventilation, rare proof of the presence of energy on board.
The spacious command post was just as deserted and lifeless. The computers remained off, the seats empty. A whole section of one of the walls could allow a view of the outside in normal times, but a solid shutter was closed. Thus, not a light from the distant stars could illuminate the interior of the room. Everything seemed dead, when suddenly, a light began to flash.
It was progressive. Various machines began to hum. Holographic screens came to life here and there. On one of them, the three-dimensional map of the ship was drawn. At the level of the rear part, which formed a gigantic sphere seeming to have been anchored in the rest of the device, a few red dots appeared...
